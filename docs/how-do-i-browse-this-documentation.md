# How do I browse this documentation?

**Welcome to the Piwigo documentation!**

Follow the guide to find answers to your questions and make the most of the information available here.

## Using the search

If you are searching for a specific piece of information, use the search available in the top left corner of the website.

![image.png](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-cced4415.png)

Simply click on the search box : from there, you just need to enter the desired text and all the contents of the documentation associated with it will appear. 

![Test recherche site.jpg](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-f070de0f.jpg)

## Using the top menu

At the top of each page, a browsing menu allows you to see where you are within the documentation, and browse through the web tree.

![image.png](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-51950906.png)

## Using the left menu

The left menu, present on all pages, lists all of the documentation's sections : photo import, gallery browsing, album management…

![image.png](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-b12a4523.png)

Use the slider bar or your mouse's scroll wheel to browse the menu on the left and view all the available sections.

Click on a section to open the drop-down menu containing all the articles in this section.

![Exemple menu tags.jpg](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-3c9e61e3.jpg)

For each section, a summary page lists all the articles of the section.

![Naviguer dans la galerie.jpg](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-10e9d3bb.jpg)

## Browsing within an article

To the right of each article, the summary allows you to view the main topics in the article and click on the one you are interested in to access its content directly. 

![An article’s summary](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-f7e3decf.jpg)

An article’s summary

## Activating dark mode

To turn dark mode on or off, click on the icon depicting a sun in the top right corner of each page.
