# Manage your favorites

How can you add photos to your favorites in Piwigo and find them again easily? In this article, we summarize all our tips.

## Add a photo to your favorites from your gallery

Users who are logged in can add a photo to their favorites from the Piwigo gallery in order to find it again later.

From a photo's page, click on the "Add this photo to your favorites" icon. In most galleries, this icon is shaped like a heart, but this may change depending on your theme.

## Finding your favorites in the gallery

To find your favorites, go to the Your favorites page.

The location of this page depends on your theme and your menu's settings, but, in most cases, it is accessible through the "Explore" menu (for the Modus theme) or "Discover" (for the Bootstrap Darkroom theme).

## Manage favorites in a batch

In the administration, the batch manager lets you apply changes in bulk to your favorites. To access the batch manager, go in the administration, in the Photos > Batch Manager menu. You can easily find your favorites again thanks to the "Your favorites" predefined filter.

![Filtre favoris.jpg](https://ressources.piwigo.com/uploads/c/v/7/cv7jpz6hf8/2026/07/03/20260703190344-3f00c874.jpg)

You can then select the photos to be modified among your favorite photos or select them all, and choose the action to be performed.

To learn more about the batch manager, [read this article](../import-and-manage-photos/batch-manager-piwigo.md).

## Quick Fav: Add a photo to the favorites from the Album page

By default, the "Add to favorites" button is only accessible from a photo's page.

The **Quick Fav** plugin allows you to add a photo with one click from its thumbnail, on the Album page or another listing page.

It also lets you add all the content of an album to your favorites in one click.

[Learn more about Quick Fav](albums-in-your-gallery.md)

## User Collections: Sort your favorites into themed collections

If you need to create multiple themed selections, the User Collections plugin is made for you!

It lets you create collections, add files to them, and share them via link or email.

[Learn more about User Collections](user-collections-plugin-piwigo.md)
